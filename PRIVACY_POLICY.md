# Privacy Policy for MealScrape

**Last Updated: November 25, 2025**

## Introduction

MealScrape ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, username, and password
- **Profile Information**: Display name, bio, avatar photo, and banner photo
- **User-Generated Content**: Recipes, posts, comments, ratings, reviews, and messages
- **Social Information**: Followers, following, and interactions with other users

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, and interaction patterns
- **Camera and Photos**: Access when you choose to upload recipe photos or videos

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our recipe sharing service
- Enable social features like following, commenting, and messaging
- Personalize your experience with recipe recommendations
- Send notifications about interactions and updates
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services
- **Recipe Extraction Services**: To import recipes from external websites

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct or update your information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of push notifications
- Control who can see your content (public/private settings)
- Export your recipes and data

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety
- With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)

## Children's Privacy

MealScrape is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications for:
- New followers and social interactions
- Comments and likes on your posts
- Direct messages
- App updates and features

You can disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

## Camera and Photo Access

We request camera and photo library access only when you:
- Upload recipe photos or videos
- Update your profile picture or banner
- Share content with images

We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit permission.

## Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:
- Personal information is removed within 30 days
- Public content may be retained in anonymized form
- Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:
- In-app notifications
- Email notifications
- Update to "Last Updated" date

## Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy:
- Email: support@mealscrape.com
- In-App: Settings > Help & Support

## Your Consent

By using MealScrape, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our data practices as described herein.
